Our company is looking for an industrial building

19.08.2022Important

Our company is looking for an industrial building for a sewing workshop in Varna.

Parameters

District: western and northern industrial zones in Varna;
Building: 150–200 meters;
Ground floor: at least 100 meters;
Network power: 20–30 kW;
Ceilings: from 2.0 meters to 3.0 meters;
Ventilation: is not essential;
Sewerage and water: are needed;
Warehouse: 50–100 meters;
Office: 2–3 rooms;

There must be a permit for production on the project.
